Problem: Preparing a Chocolate Cake

Tasks

  • Complete the algorithm to solve this problem.
  • Identify the data, the processing, and the result of this algorithm.
  • Explain the difference between an algorithm and a program.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Chocolate
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Flour

Recipe

  1. Melt the butter and the chocolate.
  2. Add the sugar and mix.
  3. Beat the eggs and pour them into the mixture.
  4. Add the flour.
  5. Pour everything into a cake pan and bake in the oven.

Algorithm

  1. Prepare the ingredients (butter, chocolate, sugar, eggs, flour...). (Data)
  2. Melt the butter and chocolate, then add the sugar and mix. (Processing)
  3. Beat the eggs and add them to the mixture with the flour. (Processing)
  4. Pour the mixture into a cake pan and put it in the oven. (Processing)
  5. The chocolate cake is ready. (Result)

Difference Between an Algorithm and a Program

An algorithm is a sequence of steps used to solve a problem.

A program is the translation of an algorithm into a language that a computer can execute.
